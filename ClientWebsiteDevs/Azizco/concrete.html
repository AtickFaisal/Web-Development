Essentially, it’s concrete on a roll.

Concrete Canvas® is part of a revolutionary new class of construction materials called Geosynthetic Cementitious Composite Mats (GCCMs). It is a flexible, concrete impregnated fabric that hardens on hydration to form a thin, durable, water proof and fire resistant concrete layer

Just Add Water

CC allows concrete construction without the need for plant or mixing equipment. CC is typically used in canal lining, bank erosion protection, flood protection and control, slope protection, embankments, ditch lining, irrigation and drainage, culvert linings, gabions, ETP drainage lining, bund lining, concrete remediation, weed suppression and so on

A Brief Introduction to Concrete Canvas

01

RAPID INSTALLATION

CC can be laid at a rate of 200m²/hour, up to 10 times faster than conventional concrete solutions.

02

EASY TO USE

Available in man-portable rolls. CC requires less plant, less personnel and no on-site mixing or measuring. Just add water

03

LOWER PROJECT COSTS

The speed and ease of installation mean CC is more cost-effective than conventional concrete, with less logistical burden

04

ECO-FRIENDLY

A low mass, low carbon technology which uses up to 95% less material than conventional solutions

Regarding soil conditions, Concrete Canvas is well designed to deal with various soil conditions even those which can suffer from differential ground movement and heave
