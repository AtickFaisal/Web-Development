Hydro-Park 1127

Hydro-Park 1127 is the new Two Post Parking Lifts developed by Mutrade. They are a kind of simple parking lifts, suitable for both commercial and residential parking purposes.
They move only vertically, so the users have to clear the ground level to get the higher level car down. The hydraulic cylinder and chains drive system makes the operation fast and smooth.
Hydro-Park 1127 can be used for both sedan & SUV.

Bi-Directional Parking

BDP series is a very popular self-parking system. It is driven by hydraulic or motor system and ran by PLC.
BDP series have 2 different models with all different floors for options; we can build from 3 floor under ground to 15 floor above ground. The equipment is available for both sedans and SUVs, the capacity is from 2000kg to 2500kg.
The biggest advantage of our BDP system is that we are now still keeping a national record of the lifting and retrieving speed.
There are different finishing (paint spray) and water proof treatment (zinc spray) available at the customer request.
