Hydro-Park 1127

Hydro-Park 1127 is the new Two Post Parking Lifts developed by Mutrade. They are a kind of simple parking lifts, suitable for both commercial and residential parking purposes.
They move only vertically, so the users have to clear the ground level to get the higher level car down. The hydraulic cylinder and chains drive system makes the operation fast and smooth.
Hydro-Park 1127 can be used for both sedan & SUV.

Hydro-Park 3230

Hydro-Park 3130 and 3230 are the new 4 Post Stacker Parking Lifts designed by Mutrade, they are the most effective way to triple or quadruple the capacity of typical parking areas.
Hydro-Park 3130 allows three vehicles to be stacked in a single parking space and Hydro-Park 3230 allows four vehicles.
It moves only vertically, so the users have to clear the underneath levels to get the higher level car down.

TPTP 2

TPTP-2 is a supplement equipment of TPP-2 Series when there is not enough height available. It is driven by hydraulic system; the structure is very simple and stable.
The equipment is only available for sedans due to its 1600 clearance height and 2000kg capacity.
There are different finishing (powder coat) and water proof treatment (hot galvanizing) available at the customer request.
